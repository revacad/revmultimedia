**Hero headline**: Graphic Design
**Hero subtext**: Build the visual language of brands, businesses, and ideas.

**Overview**:
Graphic design is not about making things look pretty. It is about making things
communicate. At Rev Multimedia, we train you to understand why design works —
not just how to use the tools — so your skills stay relevant as the industry evolves.

This course covers the full lifecycle of visual design: from understanding a brief,
to building brand systems, to delivering professional print and digital output.

**Who this is for**:
- Complete beginners who want to enter the creative industry
- Marketers and business owners who want to understand visual communication
- Creatives who are self-taught and want structured, professional training
- Anyone who wants to build a freelance or agency career in design

**What you will leave with**:
- A professional portfolio of real-world design projects
- Fluency in industry-standard tools (Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign)
- A strong foundation in typography, colour theory, layout, and branding
- The ability to take a client brief and deliver professional work independently

### Motion Graphics — Detail Page

**Hero headline**: Motion Graphics
**Hero subtext**: Give your ideas a voice. Make them move.

**Overview**:
Motion graphics sit at the intersection of design and animation. It is one of the
fastest-growing creative disciplines globally — and one of the most in-demand
skills for agencies, brands, and content creators across Africa.

At Rev Multimedia, we teach motion from first principles: timing, easing,
visual hierarchy in motion, and storytelling through animation — before we
even touch the software.

**Who this is for**:
- Graphic designers who want to add animation to their skillset
- Video creators who want to level up their post-production
- Social media content creators who want to stand out
- Anyone who wants to work in advertising, broadcasting, or digital media

**What you will leave with**:
- Proficiency in Adobe After Effects and relevant motion tools
- A showreel of animated work you can show clients and employers
- Understanding of animation principles, timing, and visual storytelling
- Skills to create title sequences, lower thirds, explainer animations, and social content

The camera captures the footage. The editor tells the story.

### FAQ Section

**Section headline**: Common questions

**Q: Who can apply to Rev Multimedia Academy?**
A: Anyone who wants to build a professional creative career. You do not need any prior experience — just commitment and a willingness to put in the work. We have students who started with zero design background and went on to work with clients professionally.

**Q: Are the courses available online?**
A: Yes. Selected cohorts for all three disciplines run fully online via Zoom. These are open to students across Ghana and internationally. Check each course page for the mode of the upcoming intake.

**Q: How does the application process work?**
A: You fill out an online application, pay a non-refundable application fee of GHS 100, and our admissions team reviews your application. If accepted, you receive a tuition invoice and payment instructions. Once tuition is paid, your enrollment is confirmed and you receive your student ID.

**Q: What is the tuition fee?**
A: Tuition fees vary by course. You will find the fee for each course on its detail page. We offer structured payment plans — speak to us if you need flexibility.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: Yes. We understand that paying a full tuition upfront is not always possible. Installment arrangements are available — contact us at info@revmultimediagh.com to discuss your situation.

**Q: Is the application fee refundable?**
A: No. The application fee covers the cost of reviewing your application and is non-refundable regardless of the outcome.

**Q: Do I need a specific laptop or software?**
A: You will need a laptop that can run Adobe Creative Cloud applications. Minimum specs and software requirements are shared with accepted students during onboarding. If you have concerns about your equipment, reach out before applying.

**Q: Does Rev Multimedia issue certificates?**
A: Yes. Students who complete a course receive an official Rev Multimedia Academy certificate. Our academic collaboration with Ghana Technology University College adds further institutional credibility to your training.
